# PlumbEasy

## Reliable Plumbing Made Easy

### Vision
PlumbEasy connects customers with verified plumbers through WhatsApp and web-based booking.

### Problem
- Customers struggle to find trusted plumbers.
- Plumbers often experience dry days without work.

### Solution
A trusted platform connecting customers and plumbers.

### Launch City
Visakhapatnam

### Future Expansion
- Electricians
- Painters
- Carpenters
- AC Technicians

### Revenue Model
5–10% commission after building a strong customer base.
